# Brief — Marrakech Travel Co

What is this site for (goal):  A Marrakech-based travel agency offering curated tours and
experiences ENTIRELY WITHIN MOROCCO (domestic travel) — Sahara desert trips, Marrakech &
imperial cities, Atlas Mountains, coastal towns, Chefchaouen. Convert visitors into tour
bookings / trip-planning inquiries.
Who is it for (audience):  Travelers (international and domestic) who want authentic, well-guided
Morocco experiences led by local experts, based out of Marrakech.
Pages needed:            One rich Home page (sections in design.md).
Primary call-to-action:  "Plan your trip" / "Book a tour"
Anything must-have:      Featured tours/experiences grid, trust signals (local experts), testimonials,
FAQ, simple contact/inquiry CTA. Everything is Morocco-only.
Anything to avoid:       Generic stocky travel clichés; international/outside-Morocco destinations.
